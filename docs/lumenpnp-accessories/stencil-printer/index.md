### **Repacking**

The following instructions will guide you through repacking the box for shipment to the customer.

![](img/repacking.webp)

**1.** Return the `Squeegee 7cm x 10cm` into the foam wrap and place on the inside of the shrink wrap that is wrapped around the `Stencil Printer`.

**2.** Return the 2x `40 Embossing Start Knob M8x14` back to the red bubble wrap bag and place on the inside of the shrink wrap that is wrapped around the `Stencil Printer`.

**3.** Return the `X-axis Adjustment Handle`, `Y-axis Adjustment Handle`, 2x `Z-axis Adjustment Handle`, and `Swing Handle` back to the red bubble wrap bag and place on the inside of the shrink wrap that is wrapped around the `Stencil Printer`.

**4.** Place the `Stencil Printer` back into the foam walls and place in a shipping box.

**5.** Return the weight for the `Balance Adjustment Device` to the square cutout in the foam.

**6.** Return the rod for the `Balance Adjustment Device` and the `Counterweight Rod Supporting Blade` to the long rectangular cutout in the foam.

**7.** Place the `User Manual` on top of the `Stencil Printer` first, then place the `Parts Box` and `Two-way Ratchet (size 10)` on top of the `User Manual`. Finally, place the completed `High-Precision Solder Paste Printer Checklist` on top and seal the box.

!!! success "All Done!" 
    The package has successfully been QCed and is ready to ship to the customer.
