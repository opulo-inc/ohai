    Removing Non-Serialized Items from Opulo Managed Inventory per Production Batch

    Export Item Removal List from ShipStation

    1. Navigate to https://ship13.shipstation.com/home and login

    2. Click "Shipments"

    3. Expand the "Batches" drop-down menu and select "Open"

    4. Click "WK27" (or the given production week # you are working with)

    5. Click "Export Orders"

    6. Click "Export order line items"

    7. Click the "Weekly Non-Serialized Item Removal" field.

    8. Click "Export" and save the file somewhere you can easily it on your computer

    Prepare sanitized item removal .CSV

    1. Navigate to Weekly Aligni Item Removals - Google Drive

    2. Make a copy of "WK ## NON-SERIALIZED ITEM REMOVAL"

    3. Right-click "WK ## NON-SERIALIZED ITEM REMOVAL"

    4. Click "Make a copy"

    5. Right-click "Copy of WK ## NON-SERIALIZED ITEM REMOVAL"

    6. Click "Rename"

    7. Click this text field.

    8. Click this text field.

    9. Type "[[Backspace]]" to delete "Copy of " from the front of the filename

    10. Replace ## with the given production lot's week number

    11. Type "27" (for example)

    12. Click "OK"

    13. Double-click "WK 27 NON-SERIALIZED ITEM REMOVAL" and Open the File

    14. Right-click "WK ## FROM SHIPSTATION"

    15. Click "Rename" on the first tab of the Workbook

    16. Click "WK ## FROM SHIPSTATION"

    17. Replace ## with the production lot's given week number – "27" in this case

    18. Click "WK ## NON SERIALIZED ITEMS TO REMOVE"

    19. Right-click "WK ## NON SERIALIZED ITEMS TO REMOVE"

    20. Click "Rename"

    21. Click "WK ## NON SERIALIZED ITEMS TO REMOVE"

    22. Replace ## with the production lot's given week number – "27" in this case

    23. Click "WK 27 FROM SHIPSTATION" to exit the renaming pop-up window

    24. Navigate to the "WK## FROM SHIPSTATION" tab

    25. Select Cell "A1"

    26. Click "File"

    27. Click "Import"

    28. Click "Upload"

    29. Click "Browse"

    30. In your computer's file explorer, navigate to and select the .CSV file you previously exported from ShipStation

    31. Click "Create new spreadsheet" and expand the drop-down menu

    32. Select "Replace data at selected cell"

    33. Select "Import data"

    34. Confirm all data imported correctly from the top to bottom

    35. Switch to the "WK ## NON SERIALIZED ITEMS TO REMOVE" tab

    36. Click on the Pivot Table

    37. Select the "Edit" pop-up button

    38. Edit the filtered "BASE SKU" list

    39. Click "Clear" to deselect all SKUs

    40. Click "Select all"

    41. Click "(Blanks)" to remove items without SKU numbers

    42. In the Filtered List search bar, type "sku-00"

    43. Click "SKU-0002" to filter LumenPnP units from the list

    44. Repeat this step, as needed, to remove any of the following items:

      • Replacement Motherboards
      • Control Boxes

    45. Click "OK"

    46. Click here to exit the Pivot table editor

    47. Click "File"

    48. Select "Download" from the File drop-down menu before selecting "Comma Separated Values (.csv)"

    Removal items from inventory in Aligni

    Up-Rev the Removal Batch Item

    1. Navigate to opulo.aligni.com

    2. Click on the search button in the top-right of the UI

    3. Type "Batch-0001" in the search bar's text field

    4. Click "BATCH-0001"

    5. Click "New Revision"

    6. Disable "Copy part list from source revision"

    7. Click the "Revision Name" field.

    8. Type "WK#27" (the given week number of the production lot you are working with)

    9. Click the Create button

    10. Click the BOM tab's drop-down menu.

    11. Click "Part List"

    12. Click "Import CSV"

    13. Click "Drag a file here to upload, or choose a file from your computer"

    14. From your computer's file explorer, navigate to and select the .CSV previously downloaded from Google Sheets

    15. Map Part Number to "Column 1 (BASE SKU)"

    16. Map QUANTITY to "Column 2 (SUM of Item - Qty)"

    17. Click "Match Parts & Move to Step 3"

    18. Perform the following two steps on this page:

      1. Select the "Add to working partlist" import option
      2. Click "Import Parts"

    19. You will be returned to the BOM (Part List) view

    20. Remove any errant items by clicking the "Red X" in the Actions column of the Part list

      • It's important to remove any items from this list that may have already been previously removed from inventory – sometimes replacement parts shipped fast can be guilty of this

    21. Click "Yes" to remove anything that shouldn't be accounted for

    22. Click "Release this Revision"

    23. Click "Release Revision"

    Make a 1pcs Build for the Removal Batch Item

    1. Click "New Build"

    2. Click "Create a single build"

    3. Click the "Title" field.

    4. Type "Build for non-serialized goods made during WK #27" (27 for example)

    5. Click the "Build Quantity" field.

    6. Type "1"

    7. Enable "DRILL-DOWN PARTLIST"

    8. Click the "Expected Reservation Date" field and set it to the day the production lot began

    9. Click the "Expected Completion Date" field and set it to the date the production lot is scheduled to be completed

    10. Click "Create Build"

    11. Click "Allocate..."

    12. Click "Allocate" from the pop-up window

    13. Click "Reserve Inventory..."

    14. Click "Reserve"

    15. Click "Parts"

    16. Confirm all material has been properly reserved before navigating to the "Finalize" tab

    17. Disable "Add final assembly to inventory"

    18. Pick "Computed Price ($####.##)" as selected "Record Cost As" option

    19. Click "Finalize Build..."

    20. Click "Finalize Build"

    21. Click "Parts"

    22. Confirm all parts have been removed
